Bread

Bread is the staff of life. Bread is all you need to survive, essentially

Ingredients

  • 150g bubbly, active starter
  • 250g warm water
  • 25g olive oil
  • 500g bread flour (not all purpose)
  • 10g fine sea salt
  • fine ground cornmeal, for dusting

Method

  1. Make the dough
  2. Bulk rise
  3. Cut & Shape the dough
  4. Score the dough
  5. Bake the dough
